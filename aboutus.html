WHO WE ARE

ZEAL MENA is a marketing consultancy and media production company. A young boutique agency based in the heart of Beirut, we work with our clients across the region to drive brand growth by providing a one-stop shop for all your media, marketing and creativity needs.

OUR HISTORY

ZEAL MENA was founded by a group of friends who have been working across different media industries for over a decade. By combining their expertise, skills and knowledge across marketing, communications and media production, the team offers comprehensive and integrated solutions that drive brand growth.

As a marketing consultancy and media production company, we are passionate about telling meaningful stories and bringing our clients’ vision to life. We do this through unique storytelling, impactful strategies and campaigns that truly resonate.

We work with various kinds of clients; from established businesses to young startups, we always ensure creative and quality content that elevates your brand to the next level.
